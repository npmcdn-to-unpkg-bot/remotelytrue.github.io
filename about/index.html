Who am I?

I'm Phil Steindel. I've got a Ph.D. in Biophysics and Structural Biology from Brandeis University, where I studied the evolution of some quasi-obscure proteins. Since then, I've been making my way into the bioinformatics and data science community, doing some projects along the way. I also love solving and (more recently) constructing puzzles, from crosswords all the way to stuff like the MIT Mystery Hunt.
